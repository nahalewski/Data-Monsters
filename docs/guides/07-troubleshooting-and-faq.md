# 7) Troubleshooting & FAQ

## Common Issues

### I can't find a record.

- Check module filters and ownership filters.
- Verify spelling in global search.
- Confirm the record wasn't archived.

### My report numbers look incorrect.

- Confirm date range and timezone.
- Review filter logic (AND/OR conditions).
- Check if pipeline stage definitions changed.

### I don't receive notifications.

- Verify profile email and notification settings.
- Check spam/junk folder.
- Ask admin to verify your role permissions.

## FAQ

### When should I convert a lead to a contact?

When qualification criteria are met and you have confirmed buyer relevance.

### Should I delete old records?

Prefer archiving to preserve history unless policy requires deletion.

### Who can edit shared reports?

Users with report edit permissions granted by administrators.

## Escalation Path

1. Confirm issue steps and screenshots.
2. Check this guide and team best-practice notes.
3. Contact internal CRM administrator.
4. Escalate to technical support with details (time, record ID, module, error text).
