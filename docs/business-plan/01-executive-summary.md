# 1) Executive Summary

## Business Overview

**Appalachian Technologies** builds and deploys AI-powered automation systems that handle lead follow-up, appointment scheduling, and customer communication for small and mid-sized businesses.

Our systems combine:

- AI voice agents
- SMS automation
- Workflow integrations across CRM and communication tools

This allows clients to contact every lead instantly and consistently, improving conversion rates and revenue without adding staff.

## Problem

Lead-driven businesses frequently lose revenue because:

- Calls are missed during peak hours and after-hours
- Lead follow-up is delayed or inconsistent
- Sales teams are overloaded with repetitive communication tasks

## Solution

Appalachian Technologies provides a turnkey automation layer that:

- Responds to inbound leads immediately
- Re-engages missed calls through voice and SMS
- Books qualified appointments automatically
- Keeps communications documented and trackable

## Current Stage

The business is early-stage with core components already prepared and validated:

- AI system architecture designed and tested
- Vapi call flows and automation workflows prepared
- Dealership-specific scripts complete
- Marketing assets and outreach workflows ready
- Client contract templates in place
