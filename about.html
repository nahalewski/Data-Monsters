About Appalachian Technologies

Business Overview

Appalachian Technologies is a business automation company serving organizations that depend on incoming leads, phone calls, appointment requests, and ongoing customer communication. We design systems that help businesses respond faster, follow up more consistently, and keep a cleaner record of sales activity.

Service Model

We operate as a recurring-revenue service partner. Clients typically pay an upfront implementation fee to customize and launch the system for their workflow, followed by a monthly service fee for maintenance, optimization, support, and continued operation. This model creates predictability while providing clear value to the client through ongoing performance improvements.

12-Month Objective

Our near-term goal is to sign and support a base of recurring clients in automotive and local service industries, prove strong case studies, and build a repeatable sales process. Over the next 12 months, Appalachian Technologies aims to establish a reliable monthly revenue base, strengthen regional market credibility, and position the company for wider expansion with a scalable service offering.
