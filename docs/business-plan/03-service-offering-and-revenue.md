# 3) Service Offering & Revenue Model

## Core Service Offering

Appalachian Technologies delivers AI automation systems that include:

- **AI voice agent workflows** for inbound/missed calls
- **Automated SMS follow-up** and nurture campaigns
- **Appointment scheduling flows**
- **CRM and workflow integrations**
- **Monitoring, optimization, and support**

## Deliverables Per Client

Typical deployment includes:

1. Discovery and workflow mapping
2. Script customization for the client’s sales/service process
3. Voice and SMS sequence setup
4. CRM/tool integration
5. Testing, launch, and optimization

## Revenue Model

### One-Time Setup Fee

- **$2,000–$5,000** per client

### Recurring Monthly Subscription

- **Standard clients:** average **$500/month**
- **High-value dealership clients:** **$750–$1,000/month**

### Additional Revenue Streams

- Custom automation builds
- Advanced CRM integrations
- Ongoing maintenance/support add-ons

## Margin Characteristics

This is a high-margin model with low fixed overhead:

- Software/API costs scale efficiently across clients
- Delivery is process-driven and repeatable
- Recurring subscription revenue compounds each month

## Strategic Focus

The company prioritizes **recurring monthly revenue growth** by combining strong onboarding execution with retained, value-based subscriptions.
