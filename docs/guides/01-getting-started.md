# 1) Getting Started

## Who This Is For

This guide is for end users who are new to the CRM Tool and need to complete core setup quickly.

## First Login Checklist

1. Sign in with your assigned company credentials.
2. Complete your profile (name, title, team, timezone).
3. Set your preferred notification channels.
4. Verify your email signature and contact details.
5. Review your team dashboard.

## CRM Layout Overview

- **Top Navigation**: Global search, notifications, quick-create.
- **Sidebar**: Main modules (Leads, Contacts, Accounts, Deals, Tasks, Reports).
- **Main Panel**: Current module list, record details, and actions.
- **Activity Feed**: Timeline of updates and interactions.

## Key Concepts

- **Lead**: A potential customer not yet qualified.
- **Contact**: A person associated with a customer or prospect.
- **Account**: A company or organization record.
- **Deal/Opportunity**: A revenue-related sales process.
- **Task**: A to-do item assigned to a user.

## Best Practices

- Keep records complete and up to date.
- Use standardized naming conventions.
- Log customer interactions the same day.
