# 4) Go-to-Market & Operations Plan

## Current Assets and Readiness

Appalachian Technologies has already prepared key growth assets:

- AI follow-up system design (voice + SMS)
- Dealership-specific call scripts and workflows
- Planned automation stack (Vapi, Twilio, Make.com)
- Facebook advertising campaign assets
- Business branding and logo
- Client contract templates
- Target market list and outreach strategy

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Paid Acquisition

- Launch Facebook advertising campaigns targeting dealerships and local service operators
- Use segmented messaging and vertical-specific offers
- Track cost-per-lead and cost-per-client acquisition weekly

### Direct Outreach

- Structured outreach to priority business categories
- Scripted pitch focused on missed-call and speed-to-lead revenue loss
- Follow-up sequences through SMS, email, and calls

### Referral and Case-Study Growth

- Use early client outcomes as proof points
- Request referrals from successful implementations
- Build a vertical portfolio to improve close rates

## Sales Process

1. Lead qualification and discovery call
2. Workflow audit and ROI positioning
3. Proposal and implementation timeline
4. Setup and launch
5. Performance review and ongoing optimization

## Operating Plan (12 Months)

- Standardize onboarding templates and scripts
- Implement KPI tracking for lead response and booking conversion
- Shorten onboarding cycle time through repeatable workflows
- Maintain service quality while expanding client count to 25–35

## Key Performance Indicators

- New clients acquired per month
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Retention rate / churn
- Lead-to-appointment conversion improvement for clients
- Average onboarding time per account
