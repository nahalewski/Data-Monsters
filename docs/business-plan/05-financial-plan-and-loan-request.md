# 5) Financial Plan & $50,000 Funding Request

## Funding Request

Appalachian Technologies is requesting **$50,000** in startup/growth capital.

## Use of Funds

| Category | Amount | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Marketing & customer acquisition | $15,000 | Facebook ads, outbound campaigns, lead generation |
| Software & API infrastructure | $10,000 | Vapi, Twilio, Make.com, supporting tools |
| Equipment & development environment | $10,000 | Workstations, systems setup, deployment infrastructure |
| Operating capital | $10,000 | 3–6 months runway for fixed and variable operating needs |
| Reserve / contingency fund | $5,000 | Scaling flexibility and unplanned costs |
| **Total** | **$50,000** |  |

## Why $50,000 Is the Right Amount

This funding level supports both acquisition and execution:

- Provides enough marketing capital to drive meaningful client volume
- Covers technical delivery capacity for rapid onboarding
- Maintains operating runway to reduce early cash-flow risk
- Includes a reserve buffer for controlled, low-risk scaling

## Loan Positioning Statement

> “The requested funding will be used to accelerate customer acquisition and scale a proven recurring revenue model. With low overhead and high-margin services, the business is positioned to achieve profitability quickly and generate consistent cash flow to support loan repayment.”

## Risk Management

- Diversified target segments (dealership + local services)
- Combination of setup fees and recurring subscriptions
- Lean cost structure with variable scaling in software spend
- Reserve capital to handle volatility and opportunity spikes
